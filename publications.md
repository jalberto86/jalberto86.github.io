## Study days & seminars

1. **2025 — GENOPOLE Research Day** (13/02/2025)  
   Participation and presentation of ongoing work on energy economics, advanced biofuels, and the low-carbon transition.

2. **2024 — APOGEEBIO & Association Bernard Gregory Workshop** (27/02/2024)  
   Participation in a career-development and research-valorization workshop; presentation of my postdoctoral project in energy economics.  
   **Methodologies:** social network analysis and multilayer networks; gravity models and international R&D collaboration (EPO and CORDIS); patent counts and negative binomial models.

3. **2022 — Technology Management, Economics and Policy Workshop (Seoul National University)** (03/2022)  
   Participation and presentation on the environmental effects of international trade and land-use change.  
   **Methodologies:** disaggregating an energy module in CGE models; creating a land supply module in GTAP-AEZ CGE models; GHG emissions accounting and social cost of carbon; CGE and welfare decomposition.
