### Overview

My work sits at the intersection of **environmental & energy economics**, **technological change**, and **international trade**.
A recurring theme is how policy (carbon pricing, standards, subsidies, trade agreements) reshapes incentives to innovate and
how those changes propagate through **global value chains**, **land-use**, and **emissions**.

### Methods

- Computable General Equilibrium (CGE) modelling (GTAP / GAMS)
- Integrated assessment and spatially-explicit optimization
- Input–output analysis
- Applied econometrics
- Innovation and patent network analysis

### Current directions

- Biofuel technologies in net-zero strategies and the role of technological spillovers
- Trade-induced land-use transitions and greenhouse gas emissions
- Innovation networks and firm knowledge bases in advanced biofuels (EU)
