## Overview
My research sits at the intersection of **environmental and energy economics**, **technological change**, and **international trade**. A central objective is to understand how climate and trade policies reshape incentives to innovate and how these changes propagate through **global value chains**, **land use**, and **emissions**. I combine **CGE/GTAP modelling**, applied econometrics, and spatially explicit and network-based approaches to connect policy design with measurable economic and environmental outcomes.
